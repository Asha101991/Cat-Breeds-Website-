Persian Cat

The Persian cat is known for its long, luxurious coat and sweet personality. They are calm, gentle, and love relaxing indoors.

Siamese Cat

The Siamese cat is famous for its striking eyes, sleek coat, and talkative personality. They are social and affectionate companions.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They are friendly, playful, and known for their fluffy tails.

Bengal Cat

The Bengal cat is known for its beautiful spotted coat and energetic personality. They are intelligent, curious, and love to play.
