Cat Breeds World

Persian Cat

The Persian cat is known for its long, luxurious coat and sweet personality. They are one of the most popular cat breeds in the world.

Siamese Cat

The Siamese cat is famous for its striking blue eyes and sleek, short coat. They are vocal and affectionate companions.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They are known for their friendly nature and distinctive tufted ears.

Bengal Cat

The Bengal cat is known for its spotted coat and energetic personality. They are intelligent and love to play.
